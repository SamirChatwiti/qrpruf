The Problem Today

Managing buyers with WhatsApp is costing you.

📱

Lost Orders

Orders sent via WhatsApp, WeChat, and email are scattered, easy to miss, and hard to track.

💬

Price Confusion

Each buyer has negotiated different prices. Mistakes happen when managed manually.

📋

Manual Invoicing

You spend hours creating and sending invoices by hand, instead of focusing on your business.

❓

No Visibility

Buyers have no way to track their shipments — they call you daily instead of checking online.

Our Solution

A private branded portal for all your buyers

✓

Each buyer gets their own account with their negotiated prices — no more manual price management.

✓

Buyers place orders online 24/7 from any device — you wake up to organized orders in your dashboard.

✓

Real-time shipment tracking for every container — buyers see their delivery status without calling you.

✓

Invoices and packing lists generated and downloadable automatically — zero admin work on your end.
