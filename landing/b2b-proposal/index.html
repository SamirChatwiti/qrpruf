Proposition 02 — Internal

Agent & Distributor Network Platform

Which agent brought you the most revenue last year? Know in 3 seconds. Automate territories, commissions, and performance tracking.

⚠ The Problem Today

You work with intermediaries across multiple countries. Commissions are tracked on Excel. Performance is discussed on WeChat. Territory conflicts arise without warning. You have no real visibility on who your best-performing agents are.

✓ Our Solution

A dedicated platform for your entire agent network. Assign territories, define commission rules by product and volume tier, track performance in real time, and automatically calculate what you owe each agent at the end of every month.

🗺️

Agent Profiles & Territories

Define who represents which products in which country or region. Clear territory rules eliminate conflicts between agents.

💰

Commission Engine

Set rules by product, category, or volume tier. Calculations are automatic — no more manual Excel work at month-end.

📊

Performance Dashboard

Real-time ranking of your agents. Monthly volume, conversion rate, and growth trends — all visible at a glance.

🏪

Agent Mini-Portal

Each agent gets their own private space to manage local clients, place orders on their behalf, and track their own earnings.

🏆

Incentives & Bonus Tracking

Set quarterly targets. When an agent exceeds their goal, a bonus triggers automatically. Motivate your network effortlessly.

🧾

Commission History

Full audit trail of all commissions — paid, pending, disputed. Every agent sees their own history. No more disputes.
